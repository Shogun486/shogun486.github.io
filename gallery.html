3D Experience (Art Institute of Chicago)

This website allows a user to search for all artwork pieces available under the Art Institute of Chicago's API.

The distinguishing features of this website are that it's "on-demand" (you decide what you want to see) and 3D (allowing you to walk/approach paintings as if you were at the Institute in-person)!

When you approach each artwork, a description box will pop up to give you more info about the piece.

Similar to how you'll find a wall of frames at the Institute, you'll see that I've mimicked this arrangement. Check out my demo and Github below!
