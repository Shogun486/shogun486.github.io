Live Train-Tracking for Chicagoans (Android App)

Through the Chicago Transit Authority's API, passengers can view all 'L' stations in Chicagoland, what rail colors they serve, the stops/branches at each station, and the arrival times for each respective run for the next hour.

The unique aspect of this app is that it includes a visualizer (something not implemented at Chicago train platforms yet), and has a user interface that resembles the design schemes of signs at physical 'L' platforms in Chicago.
