Civil Advocacy Android App

Through Google's Civic Information API, this Android app allows users to search for national, state, and local political figures by city, state, zip code, or current location.

The purpose of this app is to let people navigate the political landscape of the USA with ease. Users can quickly know which party politicians belong to, follow them on social media, and potentially even contact them.

  • View UI
  • Github

Politician Info



All relevant information of each politican is shown, accompanied by an image of their respective party (which, when clicked, will direct users to the party's webpage). All links and icons shown are clickable and properly routed.


Opening External Applications



The app will display all of the politician's active social media outlets, and can direct users to view them in the Web.
